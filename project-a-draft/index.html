Scientific Name:

PRISMA

Discovered by / at:

Itself, at the beginning

Family:

Prismatidae

Habitat:

Prisma belongs to the Numeral Realm, a dimension where numbers and laws embody physical forms.

Appearance:

The modification of the Ulam Spiral is the closest tangible representation of Prisma that can be visualized in our realm. Each node of the spiral represents an integer. Just like the universe, the creature’s body continuously expands.

Key Characteristics:

  • The prime numbers are the seeds of Prisma: when one of them is clicked, the creation of a new creature begins.

What we know so far about PRISMA? It has a critical role in creating and maintaining the balance in the universe. Through mathematical laws and the positioning of the prime numbers, Prisma ensures the underlying structures of reality remain intact. Prisma’s spiral form is no accident: it mirrors the unseen structures that allow for and are resembled in the creation of galaxies and the emergence and evolution of living creatures.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Prisma reacts to user interactions, changing the direction of its development based on the user's thoughts (mouseIsDragged). Moreover, clicking on a prime segment initiates the creation of a new creature.
